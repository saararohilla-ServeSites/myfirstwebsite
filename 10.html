Our Story

Black Pepper is a premium multi-cuisine restaurant serving authentic Indian, Chinese, and global delicacies. We are known for our rich flavors, warm ambience, and exceptional hospitality.

What We Serve

From classic Indian curries and biryanis to Chinese favorites, mocktails, and bar specials — every dish is prepared using quality ingredients and hygienic practices.
